9. Test setup and measurement
Only include companies where Google Ads activity and sales-team size are both known.
Randomly split the same ICP 50/50.
Keep the subject line, sender, cadence, domains, and sample-list offer constant.
Measure positive replies, requests for the sample, pain-confirmed replies, and meetings—not reply rate alone.
Do not claim the sales-team count in the email. Use it as hidden routing logic.
Do not attack Google Ads. Position outbound as complementary: paid search captures demand; outbound selects and opens target accounts.
